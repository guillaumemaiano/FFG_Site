# Flying Fortress Games — Visual Brief

## Premise

Flying Fortress Games belongs to the world of industrial optimism.

> Not the 1930s as they were. The 1930s as they imagined the future. The 1950s of rebuilding. The 30 Glorieuses. Compressed into a Dream: "une certaine idée de la France". But NOT just France. Bauhaus. America Cup. Ferrari GT.

## Philosophy

Engineering, craftsmanship and elegance are expressions of the same culture.

Beauty is discovered through function, proportion and discipline.
