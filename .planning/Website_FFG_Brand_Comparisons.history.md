# Flying Fortress Games — Brand Identity Comparison

## Executive Summary

This document compares the two strongest visual identities currently under consideration for Flying Fortress Games.

- **Red Salon** emphasizes craftsmanship, reflection and intellectual depth.
- **French Industrial Art Deco** emphasizes engineering, ambition and timeless elegance.

The goal is to identify a single visual identity that can remain consistent across the website, GitHub, YouTube, presentations, documentation and future games.

---

# High-Level Comparison

| | Red Salon | French Industrial Art Deco |
|---|---|---|
| **Core idea** | The office of an inventor, writer and engineer | The headquarters of an engineering company |
| **Primary emotion** | Warmth | Confidence |
| **Secondary emotion** | Reflection | Ambition |
| **Overall message** | "We think before we build." | "We build things that last." |
| **Visual references** | Library, leather chair, brass lamp, notebooks, wooden desk | Ocean liners, aviation, Art Deco architecture, engineering firms |

# Website Atmosphere

| | Red Salon | French Industrial Art Deco |
|---|---|---|
| **Overall feeling** | Calm, intimate, intellectual | Premium, structured, confident |
| **Best suited for** | Essays, philosophy, devlogs, historical projects | Company website, portfolio, recruiting, technical articles |
| **Potential weakness** | Can become too literary or nostalgic | Can become too corporate or museum-like |
| **Unique strength** | Extremely human | Extremely memorable |
