# Social Media

Flying Fortress Games does not maintain official accounts on Facebook or X.

Any account claiming to represent the studio on those platforms is unofficial unless explicitly linked from this website.

The studio deliberately limits its presence on commercial social media platforms.

This website is the canonical source of information about Flying Fortress Games. All announcements, development logs, articles, and documentation are published here first.

Everything published here remains freely accessible without an account, without recommendation algorithms, and with RSS feeds for readers who prefer open distribution.

For these reasons, we choose open standards, long-form writing, source control, and RSS over algorithmic timelines. We believe these better support thoughtful discussion, durable knowledge, and the long-term preservation of our work.

## Sustainability

Flying Fortress Games considers the current social media ecosystem to carry significant social, environmental, and economic costs.

Choosing durable publishing over algorithmic distribution is, in our view, a small but deliberate contribution toward a healthier digital ecosystem.
